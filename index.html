Kalashamala
Eco Tourism

Kalashamala Eco-Tourism in Kunnamkulam is a hidden gem that offers a unique blend of natural beauty, cultural significance, and ecological awareness. It is a destination that reconnects visitors with the environment and offers a chance to enjoy nature while preserving its delicate balance. The area is managed with a focus on sustainable tourism, featuring a sacred grove, biodiversity, and eco-tourism initiatives. Visitors can enjoy walking trails, explore ancient temples, and engage in activities that promote the use of local products. Kalashamala is suitable for families and offers eco-friendly accommodations. With its serene landscapes and commitment to nature conservation, Kalashamala stands out as a model for responsible tourism in Kerala.

Why Choose Kalasamala Eco-Tourism?

Eco-Friendly Destination

Kalasamala's focus on sustainability ensures that your visit benefits the environment and local community.

A Blend of Nature and Culture

The site offers a holistic experience, combining ecological awareness with cultural exploration.

Accessibility

Located in Kunnamkulam, Kalasamala is easily accessible by road and lies within close proximity to Thrissur and Guruvayoor.

Features of Kalasamala Eco-Tourism

Biodiversity and Sacred Grove
Kalasamala houses a sacred grove, known locally as a “Kavu,” that preserves ancient trees and plants with medicinal properties. These sacred groves are a testament to Kerala's traditional practices of conserving nature through spirituality.
Unique flora and fauna.
Home to rare and endangered plant species.
Sustainable Practices
Eco-tourism initiatives here include waste management, eco-friendly accommodations, and promoting the use of local products. Visitors are encouraged to minimize their carbon footprint while exploring the area.
Walking Trails and Nature Activities
Kalasamala offers walking trails that meander through its groves, providing breathtaking views and a close encounter with nature. Bird watching, photography, and meditative activities are popular among tourists.
Cultural Heritage
Kalasamala is not just about nature; it also showcases Kerala's rich culture and traditions through local festivals, arts, and crafts.
The Appeal of Kallazhikunnu
Located near Kalasamala, Kallazhikunnu is another attraction that complements the eco-tourism experience. Known for its panoramic hill views and lush surroundings, Kallazhikunnu is perfect for hiking and picnics. Visitors can climb the hill to enjoy stunning sunrises and sunsets, making it a favorite spot for photographers and adventure enthusiasts.
Highlights of Kallazhikunnu:
  • Scenic viewpoints.
  • A peaceful escape from urban chaos.
  • Proximity to Kalasamala makes it an easy addition to the itinerary.

Photography and Bird Watching.
